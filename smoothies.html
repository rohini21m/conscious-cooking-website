Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana*
  • 1 orange, peeled
  • Â¾ cup (180 mL) unsweetened soy milk**
  • 1 Tbsp (15 mL) almond butter

Orange 'Creamsicle' Smoothie

A classic treat growing up was an orange 'creamsicle'. This smoothie is a reminder of those creamy bars. Only 4 ingredients, and it takes less than 5 minutes to make.

Directions

Add all of the ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.

Serve and enjoy immediately.

Notes

* If the banana is not frozen, add some ice to the blender.

** Nutrition tip: aim to use plant-based milk that is fortified with calcium.

STORAGE

Smoothies Best if enjoyed immediately.
