Ingredients

Spice Mix

  • 1 tsp (3 g) garlic powder
  • 1 tsp (2 g) onion powder
  • 1 tsp (2 g) paprika powder
  • 1 tsp (3 g) chili powder
  • 1 tsp (3 g) ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Salad

  • 1½ cups (297 g) cooked brown lentils
  • ½ cup (58 g) raw walnuts, chopped
  • ¼ cup (36 g) roasted sunflower seeds, chopped
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) olive oil
  • 4 cups (140 g) romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 tomato, seeded & diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • ½ cucumber, diced
  • ½ cup (70 g) corn
  • ¼ cup (34 g) sliced black olives

Quick Gaucomole

  • 1 avocado, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • ½ small red onion, diced

Cashew lime cream

  • ½ cup (65 g) raw cashews
  • ⅓ cup (80 mL) boiling water
  • 1 lime, juiced

Additional Toppings

  • fresh cilantro
  • salsa
  • sriracha hot sauce
  • tortilla chips

Bold & Filling Lentil Taco Salad

The mixture of lentils, chopped walnuts, and sunflower seeds help to bulk up the salad, making it a hearty meal choice for warm summers when you couldn't be bothered to cook anything that might heat up the kitchen.

The addition of homemade guacamole and the cashew lime cream make it deliciously saucy and flavourful..

Directions

1.In a small jar, combine all the spices for the spice mix.

2.In a medium bowl, combine the lentils, walnuts, sunflower seeds, olive oil and 4 tsp of the spice mix. Set aside while you prepare your vegetables.

3.In a large salad bowl, add the lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, corn, and olives.

4.To make the guacamole, mash all the avocado in a bowl with a fork, then stir in the remaining ingredients. Set aside.

5.To make the cashew lime cream, combine all ingredients in a small food processor or blender and blend until smooth. Add more water if you prefer a thinner consistency.

6.To make your salad, scoop out as much of the salad base as you would like, top it with scoops of the lentil and walnut mixture, then add any desired toppings like guacamole, sriracha, salsa, cashew lime cream or fresh cilantro. Lastly, add the crushed tortilla chips and enjoy!

STORAGE

Store components separately in air-tight containers in the fridge for up to one week.
